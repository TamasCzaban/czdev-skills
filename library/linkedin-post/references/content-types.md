# Content Types & Rotation

## Rotation Order
1. hot-take
2. education
3. case-study
4. behind-the-scenes

Repeat. Track externally or by asking the user what they last posted.

---

## 1. Hot Take

**Goal:** Provoke discussion, gain visibility, seed the audience.
**Engagement:** High — contrarian views generate comments.

**Structure:**
```
[Bold contrarian claim — 1 line hook]

[Why the common belief is wrong — 2-3 lines]

[What's actually true — 2-3 lines, specific]

[The nuance / when the old way IS fine]

[CTA question — "Hot take or obvious? Tell me below."]

#hashtags
```

**CZ Dev angles:**
- "No-code isn't the problem. Staying on no-code too long is."
- "Airtable is not a CRM. It's a spreadsheet with better UI."
- "Hiring a freelancer on Upwork to build your CRM is the most expensive thing you can do."
- "Most founders don't need more features. They need their data to stop living in 6 different tools."

---

## 2. Education

**Goal:** Build authority, get saves, attract ICP.
**Engagement:** High saves, moderate comments.

**Structure:**
```
[Hook: numbered list promise OR surprising stat]

[List of 5 signs / steps / mistakes — one per line, short]

1. [Point] — [1-line explanation]
2. [Point] — [1-line explanation]
...

[Closing insight — what this means / what to do instead]

[CTA question: "Which of these is hitting you hardest right now?"]

#hashtags
```

**CZ Dev angles:**
- "5 signs you've outgrown Airtable (and what comes next)"
- "3 things founders always ask us before building a custom CRM"
- "How to know when no-code is costing you more than custom development"
- "What a production-grade CRM actually needs (vs. what Notion gives you)"

---

## 3. Case Study

**Goal:** Social proof, convert warm leads, demonstrate capability.
**Engagement:** Medium comments, high DMs and leads.

**Structure:**
```
[Hook: the problem the client had — specific, painful]

The situation:
[2-3 lines of context — what they had, why it wasn't working]

What we built:
[2-3 lines — specific tools, features, outcome]

The result:
[Quantified if possible — hours saved, errors eliminated, team unblocked]

[Lesson / takeaway for founders in the same situation]

[CTA: "If your team is dealing with [problem], reply or DM — happy to talk."]

#hashtags
```

**CZ Dev angles (use real projects):**
- BEMER Hungary CRM — device management, rental contracts, automated reminders
- Vuln Prioritization Scorer — security team CVE noise reduction

---

## 4. Behind the Scenes

**Goal:** Humanize the brand, build trust, show process.
**Engagement:** High authenticity signal, good for early-stage audiences.

**Structure:**
```
[Hook: "Here's what [relatable situation] actually looks like" OR "Nobody talks about [hard thing]"]

[The honest behind-the-scenes — what the work looks like, what's hard, what surprised us]

[What we learned or what we do differently now]

[CTA: "What does your process look like for X?"]

#hashtags
```

**CZ Dev angles:**
- "What the first week of building a client's CRM actually looks like"
- "We asked a founder to describe their data flow. It took 40 minutes."
- "Here's why we always build a 'throw-away' prototype first"
- "Building in public: what we shipped this week at CZ Dev"
