## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Founder or co-founder of a growing startup or SMB (5–50 people)
- Currently using Airtable, Notion, Bubble, Zapier, Make, or similar no-code tools
- Hitting limits: too slow, too rigid, can't scale, data leaking, manual workarounds everywhere
- Wants something that actually works — not another tool they'll outgrow in 6 months
- Budget: €3K–€15K for a project (not looking for the cheapest option)
- Geography: EU/UK preferred, open to global remote
